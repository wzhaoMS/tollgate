Tollgate
Supply-chain chokepoint intelligence for AI / semiconductor investing
Don't chase the obvious trade. Trade the supplier of the obvious trade — the chokepoint that collects a toll on the whole AI build-out — and pre-define your exit for when the fact that made it special stops being secret.

What it does

OSINT harvesters

EDGAR filings, Form 4 insider trades, customer supplier-page diffs, Federal Register, smart-money feeds.

11-step scoring

Evidence grade, capacity gap, substitution risk, govt backstop, M&A floor, insider flow, exit liquidity, catalysts.

Capacity lifecycle

Flags chokepoints whose supply/demand gap is closing — a possible exit-liquidity zone — vs. still widening.

Risk discipline

Kelly-lite sizing, pair-trade watchlists, drawdown monitor, and pre-defined exit plans.

Quick start

py -m pip install -e ".[dev]"
py -m src.cli init
py -m src.cli seed_all
py -m src.cli score
py -m streamlit run src\dashboard.py
